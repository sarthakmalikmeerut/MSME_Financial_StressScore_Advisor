# MSME Financial Health Indicators and Benchmarks

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
1. **Current Ratio**: Maintaining a healthy liquidity ratio (typically > 1.33).
2. **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)**: Ensuring cash flow is sufficient to cover debt obligations.
3. **Inventory Turnover**: Efficiently managing stock to avoid capital blockage.

## Monitoring Stress
Stress is often indicated by delays in payments to creditors, frequent overdrawals in CC/OD accounts, and declining turnover compared to previous years.

Source: General Financial Analysis and RBI Guidelines
