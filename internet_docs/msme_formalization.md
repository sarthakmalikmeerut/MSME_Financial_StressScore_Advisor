# MSME Formalization and Digital Identity

## Udyam Registration
For Priority Sector Lending (PSL) purposes, banks are guided by the classification in the Udyam Registration Certificate (URC).

## Udyam Assist Platform (UAP)
UAP facilitates the formalization of Informal Micro Enterprises (IMEs). Certificates issued through UAP are treated at par with URC for availing PSL benefits.

## GST Sahay
Invoice-based financing leveraging digital public infrastructure to enable MSMEs to get quick credit based on their GST invoices.

Source: Ministry of MSME and SIDBI
