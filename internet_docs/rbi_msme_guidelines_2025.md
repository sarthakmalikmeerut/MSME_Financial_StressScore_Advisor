# RBI MSME Priority Sector Lending (PSL) Guidelines 2025

## Targets and Classification
The "Master Directions - Reserve Bank of India (Priority Sector Lending – Targets and Classification) Directions, 2025" specify that all bank loans to MSMEs qualify for classification under PSL.

## Collateral-Free Loans
Banks are mandated NOT to accept collateral security for loans up to ₹10 lakh extended to units in the Micro and Small Enterprises (MSE) sector. This limit can be increased to ₹25 lakh with appropriate authority approval based on the unit's track record and financial position.

## Stressed MSMEs
MSMEs under stress (SMA - Special Mention Accounts) can access credit facilities under specific frameworks designed for their revival.

Source: RBI Master Directions 2025
