# SIDBI Financial Support and Schemes for MSMEs 2024-25

## Equity Support
The Union Cabinet approved equity support of ₹5,000 crore for SIDBI to enhance credit flow to MSMEs, with a focus on job creation.

## Key Schemes
1. **SCLCSS**: Special Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme continued up to FY 2025-26 for SC/ST entrepreneurs.
2. **PM Vishwakarma**: Financial assistance for traditional artisans up to 2027-28.
3. **CGTMSE**: Credit guarantee for collateral-free loans up to ₹10 crore.

## Digital Underwriting
SIDBI leverages digital public infrastructure for faster digital lending and cash flow-based financing (e.g., GST Sahay).

Source: SIDBI Annual Report and Cabinet Briefings
